Official Resolution on Data Protection & Security

01. DATA SUPREMACY & COLLECTION

At Mochearn, we take the security of our Catalysts and Vendors seriously. We collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account, participate in the J-System, or communicate with the Command Center. This includes your name, email address, payment information, and any other data required to authenticate your identity within our ecosystem.

02. HOW WE USE YOUR BLUEPRINT DATA

The data we collect is used solely to maintain the integrity of the Mochearn platform. We use it to authenticate your access to premium modules, process payments for Vendors accurately, and monitor for fraudulent activities. Your data is your passport within the Mochearn Command Center.

03. COOKIES & TRACKING PROTOCOLS

Mochearn uses "Cookies" to enhance your experience. These are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and stay logged into the Command Center. Without cookies, the interactive nature of the Mochearn website would be compromised.

04. THIRD-PARTY ACCESS & VENDORS

We do not sell your data. Mochearn is an elite system, not a data brokerage. We only share information with trusted third-party service providers (like payment processors) who are required by contract to keep your information confidential.

05. SECURITY & ENCRYPTION STANDARDS

We implement high-grade encryption to protect your data. Every piece of information sent between your device and our servers is shielded. However, no system is 100% invincible. You are responsible for keeping your password and account details secure.

06. YOUR DATA RIGHTS

As a member of the Mochearn ecosystem, you have the right to request a copy of the data we hold about you. You can also request that we delete your data if you choose to leave the platform. Once deleted, this action is final—there is no recovery.

07. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

Mochearn is a global force. Your data may be stored and processed in countries outside of your own. By using our platform, you consent to this international flow of information, knowing that we maintain high-level security across all regions.

08. IP ADDRESS & GEOLOCATION

We monitor IP addresses to prevent account sharing or unauthorized access from high-risk regions. If a login attempt occurs from a suspicious location, the Command Center will freeze the account until manual identity verification is completed.

09. BIOMETRIC AUTHENTICATION

We may implement biometric authentication through third-party integrators. Mochearn does not store your raw biometric data; we receive an encrypted "Success Token" from your device to confirm your identity securely.

10. RETENTION POLICY

For active accounts, we retain all transactional data and login logs indefinitely. If you close your account, your data is moved to our "Cold Storage" vault for 24 months before a final "Wipe Command" is issued.

11. COMMUNICATION LOGS

Every message sent through the Support Hub or Command Center is recorded for quality control and dispute resolution. These logs ensure that professional integrity is maintained across the Mochearn network.

12. ANTI-FRAUD MONITORING

Mochearn utilizes automated watchdog scripts to analyze user behavior. If the system flags your behavior as "Non-Human" or "Malicious," your hardware ID and browser fingerprint will be moved to an investigation queue.

13. CHILDREN’S DATA PRIVACY

Mochearn is for individuals over the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover a minor has provided information, we will delete it with unpitying speed.

14. AFFILIATE DATA SHARING

When interacting via an affiliate link, certain data (like username and date) is shared with the affiliate for tracking. They never see your full payment details or private security questions.

15. DEVICE FINGERPRINTING

To prevent account cloning, Mochearn collects non-identifying technical data (screen resolution, OS version) to create a "Digital Signature" for your device.

16. TRANSACTIONAL LOGGING

Every financial interaction is logged with a unique cryptographic hash. These logs serve as the ultimate truth in the event of an Official Resolution or payment dispute.

17. BEHAVIORAL ANALYTICS

We use heatmaps to see which buttons are clicked and which sections are read. This data is anonymized and used to upgrade the Mochearn experience to be more efficient.

18. BREACH NOTIFICATION

In the event of a breach, Mochearn will notify all affected users via email within 72 hours. We believe in total transparency when it comes to community safety.

19. THIRD-PARTY API INTEGRATIONS

We transmit the bare minimum data to vetted API partners for services like WhatsApp notifications. They do not have access to your private Mochearn vault.

20. DATA PORTABILITY

You can request an export of your personal data in CSV or JSON format. Note that this does not include proprietary Mochearn code or J-System blueprints.

21. GOVERNING LAW

Mochearn operates under the digital laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Any disputes will be handled through official legal channels in Osogbo, Osun State.

22. THE RIGHT TO BE FORGOTTEN

You can invoke a "Hard Delete" of your account. Within 30 days, all personal identifiers will be scrubbed from our active databases permanently.

23. NETWORK LATENCY DATA

We monitor the speed of your connection to our servers. This helps us optimize the delivery of the Mochearn blueprints so that users in low-bandwidth areas still experience "Sharp" performance without lag.

24. METADATA SCRUBBING

When you upload any images (like profile photos or proof of payment) to the Mochearn Support Hub, our system automatically scrubs the "EXIF" metadata. This removes hidden GPS coordinates or device info from your photos before they are stored.

25. SESSION TIMEOUT PROTOCOLS

To protect your account from physical unauthorized access, Mochearn logs you out after a period of inactivity. We store the "last active" timestamp to ensure that your session tokens are refreshed only when you are actively moving through the platform.

26. AI-DRIVEN THREAT DETECTION

The Mochearn ecosystem utilizes AI to scan for suspicious login patterns. If the AI detects a "brute-force" attack on your account, it will automatically lock your data behind an encrypted wall until you perform a manual 2FA reset.

27. EMAIL PIXEL TRACKING

Our official emails may contain a small pixel to tell us if the email was opened. This helps the Command Center know if our official resolutions are reaching the Catalysts or if they are being blocked by spam filters.

28. VENDOR-USER MESSAGING PRIVACY

Messages between Vendors and Catalysts are encrypted. While Mochearn administrators can access these logs for dispute resolution, they are never used for marketing or shared with external parties.

29. HARDWARE ID BANNING

If a user is officially resolved and banned for fraud, Mochearn records the hardware ID of their device. This prevents that specific device from ever accessing the Mochearn website again, even with a different email or IP.

30. CLOUD INFRASTRUCTURE SECURITY

Mochearn is hosted on secure, distributed VPS architecture. Your data is not kept in one single place; it is fragmented across secure nodes. This means even if one server goes down, the Mochearn ecosystem and your data remain standing.

31. ADVERTISING PREFERENCES

Mochearn does not display third-party ads. However, we may show you "Internal Highlights" about new J-System updates. You can opt-out of these notifications, but you may miss critical wealth-building updates.

32. FINALITY OF AGREEMENT

This Privacy Policy constitutes the final word on data within Mochearn. By clicking "Accept" or continuing to use this site, you agree to every line, every subtopic, and every rule laid out in this "Story Book" of privacy.
